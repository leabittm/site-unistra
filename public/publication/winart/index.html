Infinite friezes of affine type $D$

juil. 15, 2024·
Karin Baur
Léa Bittmann
,
Emily Gunawan
,
Gordana Todorov
,
Emine Yıldırım
· 0 min. de lecture
PDF
Résumé
In this article, we study infinite friezes arising from cluster categories of affine type D and determine the growth coefficients for these friezes. We prove that for each affine type D, the friezes given by the tubes all have the same growth behaviour.
